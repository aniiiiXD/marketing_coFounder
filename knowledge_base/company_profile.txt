Eterna - Company Profile

Company Name: Eterna
Industry: Financial Technology (FinTech) - Trading Infrastructure
Stage: Series A Startup
Team Size: 15 employees
Funding Status: Series A completed

Mission Statement:
Democratizing advanced trading capabilities by providing a unified trading terminal that serves both retail traders and institutional high-frequency trading firms.

Vision:
To become the leading platform that bridges the gap between retail and institutional trading, making sophisticated trading tools accessible to everyone while providing enterprise-grade infrastructure for professional traders.

Core Values:
- Accessibility: Making advanced trading tools available to retail users
- Performance: Delivering institutional-grade speed and reliability
- Innovation: Continuously pushing the boundaries of trading technology
- Transparency: Providing clear, honest trading experiences
- Inclusivity: Serving traders of all experience levels and scales

Company Overview:
Eterna is a fast-growing fintech startup that has successfully completed Series A funding. With a lean but highly skilled team of 15 employees, we're building the next generation of trading infrastructure. Our unified platform serves two distinct but complementary markets: retail traders seeking professional-grade tools and high-frequency trading firms requiring robust, automated trading solutions.

Key Differentiators:
- Unified platform serving both retail and institutional markets
- Advanced perpetual options trading capabilities
- Comprehensive SDK suite for algorithmic trading
- Institutional-grade infrastructure with retail-friendly interface
- Series A backing providing stability and growth capital
- Agile startup culture enabling rapid innovation and deployment
