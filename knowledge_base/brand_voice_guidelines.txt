Eterna Brand Voice and Messaging Guidelines

BRAND PERSONALITY:
Professional yet Accessible | Innovative | Trustworthy | Empowering | Technical Excellence

CORE BRAND VOICE ATTRIBUTES:

1. PROFESSIONAL YET ACCESSIBLE
- Use clear, jargon-free language when possible
- Explain complex concepts in understandable terms
- Maintain professional credibility while being approachable
- Balance technical accuracy with readability

Example: "Our perpetual options engine processes thousands of trades per second" 
NOT: "Our ultra-sophisticated algorithmic infrastructure leverages cutting-edge computational paradigms"

2. INNOVATIVE
- Emphasize cutting-edge technology and forward-thinking solutions
- Highlight unique features and capabilities
- Position as industry leader in unified trading platforms
- Showcase startup agility and rapid development

Example: "We're pioneering the future of unified trading platforms"
NOT: "We offer traditional trading solutions"

3. TRUSTWORTHY
- Emphasize security, reliability, and regulatory compliance
- Use concrete facts and metrics
- Acknowledge challenges honestly
- Build confidence through transparency

Example: "99.99% uptime with enterprise-grade security"
NOT: "Probably reliable most of the time"

4. EMPOWERING
- Focus on enabling user success and growth
- Emphasize democratization of professional tools
- Highlight educational and community aspects
- Position users as capable and intelligent

Example: "Unlock professional trading strategies with institutional-grade tools"
NOT: "Let us handle your trading for you"

5. TECHNICAL EXCELLENCE
- Demonstrate deep technical knowledge
- Use precise terminology when appropriate
- Highlight performance metrics and capabilities
- Show understanding of complex trading concepts

Example: "Sub-millisecond execution with real-time Greeks calculation"
NOT: "Really fast trading with good math"

TONE VARIATIONS BY AUDIENCE:

FOR RETAIL TRADERS:
- Encouraging and educational
- Focus on accessibility and empowerment
- Use analogies and examples
- Emphasize learning and growth opportunities

FOR HFT FIRMS:
- Technical and precise
- Focus on performance and reliability
- Use industry-specific terminology
- Emphasize infrastructure capabilities

FOR PROFESSIONAL TRADERS:
- Balanced technical and accessible
- Focus on advanced features and flexibility
- Acknowledge expertise while offering value
- Emphasize efficiency and competitive advantage

MESSAGING FRAMEWORKS:

PROBLEM-SOLUTION FRAMEWORK:
"Traditional trading platforms force you to choose between retail accessibility and institutional performance. Eterna eliminates this compromise with our unified platform."

FEATURE-BENEFIT FRAMEWORK:
"Our perpetual options engine [FEATURE] enables sophisticated hedging strategies [BENEFIT] that were previously only available to institutional traders [VALUE]."

BEFORE-AFTER FRAMEWORK:
"Before: Managing multiple platforms for different trading needs. After: One unified terminal for all your trading requirements."

KEY MESSAGING PILLARS:

1. UNIFIED PLATFORM
"One platform, every trading need"
"Unified trading for retail and institutional markets"
"Eliminate platform fragmentation"

2. PERPETUAL OPTIONS SPECIALIZATION
"Advanced perpetual options made accessible"
"Professional options trading for everyone"
"Perpetual options expertise you can trust"

3. INSTITUTIONAL GRADE, RETAIL FRIENDLY
"Professional tools, accessible pricing"
"Institutional performance, retail simplicity"
"Enterprise reliability, startup innovation"

4. COMPREHENSIVE AUTOMATION
"From manual trading to full automation"
"Complete SDK ecosystem for developers"
"Algorithmic trading made simple"

WORDS TO USE:
- Unified, integrated, comprehensive
- Professional, institutional-grade, enterprise
- Accessible, user-friendly, intuitive
- Advanced, sophisticated, cutting-edge
- Reliable, secure, compliant
- Innovative, pioneering, revolutionary
- Empowering, enabling, democratizing

WORDS TO AVOID:
- Complicated, complex (use "sophisticated" instead)
- Cheap (use "cost-effective" or "competitive")
- Basic, simple (use "streamlined" or "intuitive")
- Revolutionary (overused, use "innovative")
- Best, ultimate, perfect (use specific metrics)

CONTENT STYLE GUIDELINES:

HEADLINES:
- Clear and benefit-focused
- Include key differentiators
- Use active voice
- Keep under 60 characters for digital

BODY COPY:
- Lead with benefits, support with features
- Use bullet points for complex information
- Include specific metrics and examples
- End with clear calls-to-action

TECHNICAL CONTENT:
- Define terms on first use
- Use consistent terminology
- Include practical examples
- Balance detail with readability

SOCIAL MEDIA:
- Conversational but professional
- Include relevant hashtags
- Use visuals to explain concepts
- Engage with community discussions
