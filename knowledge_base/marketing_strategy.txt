Eterna Marketing Strategy 2024

MARKETING OBJECTIVES:
1. Build brand awareness in retail and HFT trading communities
2. Generate qualified leads for both market segments
3. Establish thought leadership in perpetual options trading
4. Drive user acquisition and platform adoption
5. Support Series A growth targets and metrics

TARGET METRICS:
- 10,000+ retail trader signups in Year 1
- 50+ HFT firm partnerships
- 1M+ monthly website visitors
- 25% month-over-month user growth
- 40% conversion rate from trial to paid

MARKETING STRATEGY PILLARS:

1. CONTENT-DRIVEN THOUGHT LEADERSHIP  
Approach:
- Educational content about perpetual options trading
- Technical deep-dives for HFT audience
- Market analysis and trading insights
- Platform tutorials and best practices

Content Types:
- Weekly blog posts on trading strategies
- Technical whitepapers for institutional audience
- Video tutorials and webinars
- Podcast appearances and speaking engagements
- Social media educational content

Distribution Channels:
- Company blog and website
- LinkedIn and Twitter
- Trading forums and communities
- Industry publications and conferences
- YouTube and educational platforms

2. COMMUNITY-CENTRIC MARKETING
Approach:
- Build engaged communities around trading education
- Foster connections between retail and professional traders
- Create user-generated content and success stories
- Develop ambassador and referral programs

Tactics:
- Discord/Telegram trading communities
- Reddit engagement in trading subreddits
- User success story campaigns
- Trading competitions and challenges
- Community-driven feature development

3. PARTNERSHIP AND INTEGRATION MARKETING
Approach:
- Strategic partnerships with complementary fintech companies
- Integration partnerships with trading tools and platforms
- Affiliate programs with trading educators
- Collaboration with market makers and liquidity providers

Partnership Types:
- Trading education platforms
- Portfolio management tools
- Market data providers
- Risk management solutions
- Regulatory and compliance services

4. PERFORMANCE AND GROWTH MARKETING
Approach:
- Data-driven acquisition campaigns
- Conversion optimization and funnel analysis
- Retargeting and lifecycle marketing
- Referral and viral growth mechanisms

Channels:
- Google Ads (search and display)
- LinkedIn advertising for HFT segment
- Twitter and Reddit advertising
- Retargeting campaigns
- Email marketing automation

CHANNEL STRATEGY BY AUDIENCE:

RETAIL TRADERS:
Primary Channels:

Secondary Channels:

HFT FIRMS:
Primary Channels:

Secondary Channels:

CONTENT MARKETING CALENDAR:

WEEKLY CONTENT:
- Monday: Market analysis and insights
- Wednesday: Educational trading content
- Friday: Platform updates and features

MONTHLY THEMES:
- January: New Year trading strategies
- February: Risk management focus
- March: Options strategies deep-dive
- April: Tax season trading considerations
- May: Summer trading preparation
- June: Mid-year portfolio review
- July: Volatility trading strategies
- August: Back-to-school trading education
- September: Q4 preparation strategies
- October: Earnings season trading
- November: Year-end tax strategies
- December: Year in review and 2025 outlook

CAMPAIGN STRATEGIES:

LAUNCH CAMPAIGN: "Trading Unified"
- Multi-channel campaign introducing unified platform concept
- Focus on eliminating platform fragmentation
- Target both retail and HFT audiences simultaneously
- 3-month campaign with progressive feature reveals

EDUCATION CAMPAIGN: "Options Mastery"
- Comprehensive perpetual options education series
- Progressive skill-building content
- Community challenges and competitions
- Partnership with trading educators

GROWTH CAMPAIGN: "Refer and Earn"
- User referral program with trading credits
- Tiered rewards for successful referrals
- Social sharing and viral mechanics
- Community leaderboards and recognition

RETENTION CAMPAIGN: "Power User Program"
- Advanced features for high-volume traders
- Exclusive educational content and events
- Direct access to development team
- Beta testing opportunities

MEASUREMENT AND ANALYTICS:

KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS:
- Website traffic and engagement metrics
- Lead generation and conversion rates
- User acquisition cost (CAC) by channel
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Brand awareness and sentiment tracking
- Community engagement and growth
- Content performance and reach
- Partnership and referral metrics

ATTRIBUTION MODEL:
- Multi-touch attribution across channels
- Customer journey mapping and analysis
- Cohort analysis for retention tracking
- A/B testing for optimization

BUDGET ALLOCATION:
- Content Marketing: 30%
- Paid Advertising: 25%
- Community Building: 20%
- Partnerships: 15%
- Events and PR: 10%

COMPETITIVE MONITORING:
- Regular competitor analysis and benchmarking
- Social media sentiment tracking
- Feature comparison and positioning updates
- Market share and growth tracking
- Customer feedback and satisfaction monitoring
