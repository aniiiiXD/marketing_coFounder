    *   **Strengths:** Strong options focus, educational content, retail-friendly pricing, good mobile app.

    *   **Eterna Advantage:** Perpetual options specialization, comprehensive HFT SDK, global market access, advanced automation.

    *   **Strengths:** Advanced charting/analysis, strong educational resources, large retail base, comprehensive options.
    *   **Weaknesses:** No institutional/HFT, limited API, higher costs for active traders, complex for beginners.

    *   **Weaknesses:** Crypto-only focus, regulatory uncertainty, limited traditional options, no equity/commodity access.

*   **Bloomberg Terminal/Refinitiv Eikon:**
    *   **Strengths:** Comprehensive market data, professional adoption, advanced analytics, global coverage.
    *   **Weaknesses:** Extremely high costs, complex UI, limited retail accessibility, data-focused over execution.

*   **QuantConnect/Alpaca:**
    *   **Strengths:** Strong algorithmic trading focus, good API/SDK, developer-friendly, educational.
    *   **Weaknesses:** Limited retail interface, basic options, no perpetual options focus, primarily US markets.
    *   **Eterna Advantage:** Unified retail/algorithmic, advanced perpetual options, global access, professional trading interface.

**Eterna's Core Competitive Advantages:**

*   **Unique Positioning:** The only platform unifying retail and HFT markets with a deep specialization in perpetual options.

**Geographic Priorities:**
